Support the Mission of SUCCF

Southern Utah Chinese Christian Fellowship is a volunteer-led, faith-supported ministry. Your generous gifts help us:

  • Rent our meeting space
  • Support our children's program
  • Support SUU Chinese student club
  • Host community events (Thanksgiving, Lunar New Year, etc.)
  • Serve local Chinese immersion teachers and families
  • Reach out to Chinese students and new immigrants

How to Give

Currently, we are accepting donations in the following ways:

In Person

You may give during our Saturday fellowship (offering box available).

By Check

Please make checks payable to: SUCCF and mail to our address or give in person.

Online (coming soon)

We are working on setting up secure online giving. Please check back or contact us for updates.

Every gift, no matter the size, makes a difference. Thank you for partnering with us to build a Christ-centered, culturally connected community in Southern Utah.

SUCCF is a registered religious organization. Donations are tax-deductible as permitted by law.

Your Gift Matters

Together, we are building a Christ-centered community in Southern Utah.
