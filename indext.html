Welcome to SUCCF

Southern Utah Chinese Christian Fellowship is a growing community of believers committed to studying God's Word,
encouraging one another in faith, strengthening families, and sharing the Gospel throughout Southern Utah.

中文团契 · Chinese Fellowship

Saturday 2:00 – 3:30 PM

Join us for Bible discussion, worship songs, and sharing life together. We meet every Saturday at 475 W Buena Vista Blvd.

儿童主日 · Children's Program

Saturday 2:00 – 3:30 PM

Your children will enjoy bilingual Bible teaching from certified Chinese Immersion teachers. It’s a wonderful opportunity to learn Chinese, English, and Scripture together.
